Extract from a kathak recital

The dancers are Nina and Yulia Bagdasarova, Russian students of Guru Shri Parveen Gangani. The choreography and music are also composed by Guruji Shri Parveen Gangani.


Guruji Parveen Gangani's family belongs to the Jaipur gharana/school, however, he learnt dance from his brother Pt. Manish Gangani of the Jaipur Gharana and Pt. Birju Maharaj ji of lucknow Gharana.


The style of this kathak recital is in reality a blend of both schools.
